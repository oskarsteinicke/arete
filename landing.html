I've been stuck on bench press for 3 weeks at 185 lbs. What should I do?
Plateaus at your stage usually come down to one of three things: volume, frequency, or recovery. Since you're on PPL you're benching 2–3x a week which is solid — I'd try adding a pause rep variation or a top set with 2–3 back-off sets to break the adaptation...
What should I eat before my push day?
With a 2,500 cal goal, aim for 500–700 cal about 90 minutes out. A good combo: oats + banana + protein shake — fast carbs for energy, protein to limit muscle breakdown...
